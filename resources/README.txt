This directory lists resources (textures) used by the shaderpack. Add your own assets here: blue-noise.png, caustics.png, dudv.png, lut.png, perlinNoise.png, normal_map_examples.png

- blue-noise.png: small tiling blue noise (32x32..128x128) for dithering & stochastic sampling
- caustics.png: tiling caustic sheet used for blending underwater highlights
- dudv.png: dudv map for water perturbations
- lut.png: 3D LUT as a 2D strip for color grading (optional)
- perlinNoise.png: tiled 2D slices or packed 3D noise layers used by clouds

Place appropriately named textures in this folder or point the shader uniforms to your resourcepack textures.
